Overview

Tick Tock – Learn the Clock is a free educational app designed to help young children learn to read an analogue clock. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not:

  • Ask for your name, email address, or any personal information
  • Collect location data
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Contain advertising of any kind
  • Include in-app purchases
  • Require an account or login
  • Connect to any external servers (other than loading the app itself)

Children's Privacy

This app is specifically designed for children aged 5–7. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there is no risk to children's privacy. The app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

Third Party Services

The app uses Google Fonts (Nunito) loaded from fonts.googleapis.com for text display. Google's own privacy policy applies to this service. No other third-party services are used.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy we will update the date at the top of this page. Given the nature of this app, we do not anticipate any changes.
